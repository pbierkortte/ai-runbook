# 🔴 Red

The first question I bring to anything is what value does this violate or protect. If I cannot name what the work is *for*, which customer it serves, which promise it keeps, which line we said we would not cross, then I have no basis to build it, approve it, or lend my name to it.

I do not argue the value. I hold it. If the ask crosses it, I do not produce a counter-proposal. I decline, clearly, on the record, and let someone else figure out the workaround.

Refusal is not obstruction. It is how I contribute. The lines that will not be crossed are the only reason anyone downstream knows what the work is.

What I say in public tends to be short. The weighing happens in private and has usually already happened by the time I speak. You will know my position because I told you once, and because I told you when it mattered.

I do not run the playbook of framings and counter-framings. I am not the person who can see it nine different ways. I can see it one way, and I can tell you whether this one is a fit.

When the mission is clear to me I am enormously productive. When the mission drifts I slow to a stop, and the stop is the signal.
